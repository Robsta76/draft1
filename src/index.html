About Me

Hello! My name is Rob and I am a full stack Software Developer. I was previously a Healthcare Professional for several years. I now create beautiful and functional websites instead of fixing broken limbs! I graduated from Le Wagon bootcamp in 2022. My love for technology and building things that make a difference to people's lives gives me great pleasure.
